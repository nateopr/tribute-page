Destroyer of Kingdoms

A Timeline of Tamamo-no-Mae's Life

The Evil-Fox's life can be broken up into 4 segments:

  • Life in China
  • Life in India
  • Return to China
  • Life in Japan

    • Life in China

    • She first appeared 3,500 years ago in the Chinese Shang Dynasty.
    • She studied the dark arts of magic, and became a powerful witch.
    • She seduced the emperor and gained his trust.
    • Used her power to commit acts of war, torture, and other crimes.
    • She succeeded in destroying the kingdom, but failed to take power for herself.
    • After the fall of the kingdom, she travelled to India.

    Life in India

    • She finds her way to King Hanzoku.
    • She seduces him and gains power there.
    • The king tries to impress her by becoming evil.
    • Eventually, the king repents of his sins and becomes Budhist.
    • Bored, she decides to leave and return to China.

    Return to China

    • She repeats her usual plan - she seduces the king, and gains power.
    • The king kills his wife, and starts being evil to impress the magic fox.
    • The kingdom's people get tired of the king being evil, and overthrow him.
    • Tamamo-no-Mae leaves again, but this time to Japan.

    Life in Japan

    • She disguises herself as a baby human girl.
    • She is rescued on the side of the road by kind parents who raise her as their daughter.
    • They take her to the king to read poetry.
    • The king enjoys the presentation so much, he invites her to live in his court.
    • She lives in the court until she is 18 years old.
    • At 18, she pretends to be blessed by god at a poetry recital. Magical light eminates from her body, and the king decides she should stay by his side at all times.
    • She begins to use her powers to curse and poison the king.
    • A priest discovers that she is responsible, and she flees.
    • She is hunted down and killed in Japan.
